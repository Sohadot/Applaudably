About

Studio-level design.
Template-level price.

Apllaudably exists for the founder building a beautiful brand on a small budget.

Apllaudably is an independent design studio creating premium Canva brand kits for small beauty, wellness and lifestyle brands. We design the visual foundations — Instagram templates, brand starter kits, moodboards, launch campaigns — so that a one-person brand can look as considered as a company with an in-house design team.

Why beauty, wellness & lifestyle?

Because in these markets, aesthetics are not decoration — they are the product's first promise. A skincare line with an inconsistent feed loses trust before anyone reads the ingredients. A salon with clumsy price lists undercuts its own prices. We design for the industries where looking premium directly changes what customers are willing to pay.

What makes our kits premium?

Most template shops chase volume: hundreds of generic layouts, decorated with trendy elements. We do the opposite. Every Apllaudably kit is a small, complete system — a restrained palette, one or two carefully paired typefaces, layouts built on real editorial grids, and generous negative space. The kind of decisions a brand designer would charge four figures for, packaged so you can apply them in an afternoon.

How it works

Every kit is delivered instantly through our Etsy shop as Canva template links. You need only a free Canva account: open the link, replace the placeholder text and images with your own, adjust the colors if you wish, and publish. No software to install, no design experience required.

Start with one kit. If your feed doesn't look more expensive within a week, we haven't done our job.
