From purchase to published post.

Six simple steps. If you can use Instagram, you can use these templates.

  • 1. Purchase on Etsy. Complete checkout on the kit's Etsy listing. Delivery is instant — no waiting for an email.
  • 2. Download the PDF file. On Etsy, go to You → Purchases and Reviews → Download Files. The PDF contains your template links and quick-start guide.
  • 3. Open the Canva template link. Click the link in the PDF. It opens in Canva — a free account is all you need.
  • 4. Click "Use template". This copies the entire kit into your own Canva account. The copy is yours permanently; you can never break the original.
  • 5. Edit text, colors, and photos. Click any element to change it. Swap the placeholder photos for your own, drop in your logo, adjust the palette if you wish.
  • 6. Export as PNG/JPG/PDF. Click Share → Download in Canva, choose your format, and post. PNG is best for Instagram.

Common hiccups

The link asks me to log in. Create a free Canva account first (canva.com), then open the link again.

I can't find my download on Etsy. Make sure you're logged into the same Etsy account you purchased with, then check You → Purchases and Reviews. Purchases made as a guest arrive by email instead.

I edited something and want the original back. Open the template link from the PDF again and click "Use template" — you get a fresh, untouched copy every time.

Still stuck? Contact us or message us on Etsy — we'll walk you through it.
