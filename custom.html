Custom Requests

When a template isn't quite enough.

Template customization for beauty, wellness, salon, and grooming brands — done by the designer who built the kits.

Every Applaudably kit is designed to be self-serve. But sometimes you want it done for you — your colors applied, your logo placed, your content dropped in — or you need something the catalog doesn't cover yet. That's what custom requests are for.

What you can request

  • Color adaptation — we recolor an entire kit to your exact brand palette.
  • Logo placement — your logo positioned consistently across every template.
  • Content adaptation — your services, prices and copy placed into the layouts.
  • Custom mini-kit — a small set of templates designed around one specific need.
  • Full brand/social kit — a complete custom system built for your brand from scratch.

How it works

Email us with your brand name, what you need, and any links (Instagram, website, Etsy shop). We'll reply within one business day with a scope, a price, and a timeline. Small adaptations are usually delivered within a few days; full custom kits are quoted per project.
