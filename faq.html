FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything buyers usually ask before their first kit — answered honestly.

Do I need Canva Pro?

No. Every kit is built to work with a free Canva account. If a template ever uses a Pro element, we say so clearly on the product page — and we always provide a free alternative.

How do I receive the templates?

Instantly. After purchase, Etsy gives you a PDF download that contains your Canva template links plus a quick-start guide. Open the link, and the templates are added to your own Canva account. See Download Help for the step-by-step.

Can I change the colors and fonts?

Yes — everything is editable: colors, fonts, images, text, layouts. The kit gives you a disciplined starting system; you make it yours. Each kit includes a palette and font-pairing guide if you'd rather keep our art direction.

Can I use this for a client?

Yes. Designers and social media managers can use a kit for one client brand per purchase. You deliver the finished designs, not the template links. Full details in the License.

Are the images included?

The photos you see in previews are placeholders to demonstrate the layouts. You replace them with your own product photos, or with images from Canva's built-in free photo library, in one click.

Are the brand names and testimonials in previews real?

No. All brand names, product images, testimonials, before/after examples, and claims shown in previews are placeholders for demonstration only. Buyers are responsible for replacing them with their own accurate brand content.

Can I get a refund after download?

Because these are instant digital downloads, all sales are final once the file has been delivered — this is standard for digital products on Etsy. That said, if something is genuinely wrong with your kit (a broken link, a missing file), message us on Etsy and we will fix it or make it right.

How do I access the files after purchase?

On Etsy: go to You → Purchases and Reviews, find your order, and click Download Files. The PDF with your template links stays available there permanently — you can re-download it anytime, on any device.

Still unsure?

Ask us directly — we'd rather answer ten questions than have you buy the wrong kit.
