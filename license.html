License & Usage Terms

Plain-language rules for every Applaudably template kit. One purchase, one brand, full creative freedom within it.

You may

  • Use the templates for your own brand or a client brand.
  • Edit colors, fonts, images, and text.
  • Publish the edited designs on social media.
  • Export and print the edited designs for your brand's marketing.

You may not

  • Resell the original Canva templates.
  • Share the template links publicly.
  • Claim the template system itself as your own product.
  • Sell lightly edited versions as another template kit.

Client work

Designers and social media managers are welcome to use a kit for a client's brand. Each purchase covers one end brand — if you serve multiple clients, please purchase a copy per client. What you deliver to the client is the finished, edited designs, never the template links themselves.

Placeholder content

All brand names, product images, testimonials, before/after examples, and claims shown in previews are placeholders for demonstration only. Buyers are responsible for replacing them with their own accurate brand content.

Questions?

If your use case isn't covered here, just ask before you buy — contact us and we'll answer within a business day.
